I love traveling and reading. I’ve found a way to combine the two by picking up a book written by a local author in every new city I visit. I started this ‘tradition’ when I impulsively went into a bookstore in Barcelona and asked them to recommend a story written by a local author. That story was The Shadow Of The Wind by Zafon. I wasn’t too sure I would like this genre but the librarian seemed to genuinely love the book. I’ve never been so happy to be wrong: it was a story within a story that referenced itself. I can only describe it as an ode to storytelling and it made me realize that I need to diversify my reading diet. Hence this tradition.
